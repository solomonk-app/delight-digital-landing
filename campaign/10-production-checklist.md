# 10 · Production Checklist, Brand Kit & Testing Plan

---

## Brand kit (reuse every video)

- **Backgrounds:** cream/ivory `#F2EEE6` · espresso `#2B2320`
- **Accent:** terracotta / dusty-rose (single signature accent)
- **Type:** editorial **serif** for headlines (italic accent on the last word) · clean **sans** for body · **monospace** for labels & prompt boxes
- **Prompt card:** dark rounded box, monospace text, `[brackets]` in terracotta
- **End card:** logo dot + "AI, Made Friendly" + the video's CTA + "link in bio"
- **Feel:** calm, spacious, unhurried. Captions always on.
- **Aspect:** 1080×1920 (9:16). Keep text/CTA out of the bottom ~15% and right-side UI safe zones.

---

## Per-video production checklist
- [ ] One idea only; matches its pillar and format
- [ ] Hook lands in <2 seconds
- [ ] VO is 80–110 words; on-screen text supports (doesn't duplicate) it
- [ ] Every example prompt keeps personal data as `[brackets]`
- [ ] Tools named generically; **no unverified prices/model names**
- [ ] Any medical/legal/financial angle framed as **prep for a professional**
- [ ] Relatable/fear videos end on capability, not anxiety
- [ ] Rotated CTA (not same as previous video)
- [ ] Captions burned in; safe zones clear
- [ ] End card + correct link/handle
- [ ] Claims traced to `00-product-audit.md` (no invented features)

## Batch workflow (solo creator)
1. **Sunday:** pick the week's 5 videos from the calendar; open their `scripts/day-XX.md`.
2. **Batch by format:** record all screen-captures together; all before/after cards together; all text-led cards together.
3. **Voiceover in one sitting:** read all 5 scripts back-to-back.
4. **Edit on the shared canvases** (title/content/end cards already built).
5. **Export per platform**, adjust the 7 levers (`07-platform-adaptations.md`).
6. **Schedule 5/week (Mon–Fri)**; keep weekends for replies + next batch.

## Weekly A/B testing plan (change one variable at a time)

| Week | Variable under test | How |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Hooks | Same topic, two hook styles (permission vs. question) |
| 2 | Video length | ~25s vs. ~40s cut of similar content |
| 3 | Educational vs. promotional | Same asset taught vs. shown-as-product |
| 4 | PDF mockup vs. site screen-record | Same lesson, two visual formats |
| 5 | Voiceover vs. text-only | Same script, VO vs. silent text cards |
| 6 | CTA wording | Two product-CTA variants |
| ongoing | Parenting vs. general | Same structure, parent-specific vs. general |
| ongoing | Caregiving vs. general | Same structure, caregiver-specific vs. general |
| ongoing | Emotional reassurance vs. practical demo | Two treatments of the same idea |

Log every post in `09-tracking-template.csv` (one row per platform). Give a variant a fair sample (aim ~3+ posts) before judging.

---

## Decision rules (what to do with each video)
- **High saves, low clicks** → repost with a stronger/clearer CTA.
- **Strong 3-sec hold, weak completion** → remake with tighter pacing / shorter.
- **A topic that overperforms** → expand into a series.
- **High clicks + comments asking to buy** → make a direct-promo cut.
- **Consistently weak across a fair test** → discontinue; reallocate.
- **A hook that beat its pair** → reuse that hook shape on new topics.

Leading indicators (act early): 3-sec hold, saves, shares. Lagging (confirm): profile visits → link clicks → purchases.
