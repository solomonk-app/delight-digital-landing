# 08 · Conversion Path

**Short video → profile → link in bio → rebranded funnel page → Whop checkout → delivery (PDFs + passcode/entitlement).**

The link in bio is the **rebranded funnel page** (see `../app/page.tsx`, Workstream B) — the calm cream/serif landing page. It warms up low-intent viewers, captures free-guide emails, then hands off to Whop for purchase.

> ⚠️ The apex domain `delightdigital.online` is a **different business** (a creator/agency studio). It must never be the link in bio.

---

## The journey, stage by stage

| Stage | What the viewer does | What must be true |
|---|---|---|
| Video | Watches a reel/short | Hook lands in <2s; one clear takeaway; a rotated CTA |
| Profile | Taps the handle | Bio names the promise ("AI, made friendly — for beginners") + one link |
| Link in bio | Taps `guide.delightdigital.online` | Points to the rebranded funnel page (or a link hub → it) |
| Funnel page | Reads, maybe grabs the free guide | Calm brand match; explains the bundle; free-guide capture present |
| Whop checkout | Buys | Clear price `$19.99`, `Whop's standard refund policy`, what's included |
| Delivery | Gets access | `instantly through Whop after checkout`; passcode for `ai.`, entitlement for `workbook.` |

**Two on-ramps** on the funnel page: (1) **free guide** via MailerLite (low commitment, nurtures by email), (2) **buy the bundle** via Whop (high intent). Educational videos point to the free guide or profile; product videos point to the bundle.

---

## What the funnel/sales page must explain

- ✅ This is a **complete bundle**, not an ebook.
- ✅ The **75-page core workbook** (interactive + printable).
- ✅ Exactly which bonuses are included: 155 Parent Prompts, 18 Caregiver Prompts, "Which AI for Which Task," Permission Slip, Quick-Start Guide, Quick Reference & Tool Guide.
- ✅ That the older 52-page edition is **absorbed** (its best parts become the Quick Reference & Tool Guide), not double-shipped.
- ✅ What each website does and **how they differ** (`ai.` = reference; `workbook.` = fillable practice).
- ✅ Whether access is permanent or time-limited → `lifetime (yours to keep)`.
- ✅ How access is delivered → `instantly through Whop after checkout`.
- ✅ **Device compatibility** (responsive — verified on both sites).
- ✅ **Progress saves on your device** (verified — say "on your device," not "syncs across devices").
- ✅ **Login model** (passcode for `ai.`; purchase entitlement for `workbook.`).
- ✅ Who it's for / what it does **not** promise.
- ✅ Disclaimers: AI can be wrong (verify); privacy (never paste secrets); **educational, not medical/legal/financial advice**.

Price **$19.99** launch offer (regularly **$24.99**) · refund via **Whop's standard refund policy** · **lifetime** access · delivered **instantly through Whop after checkout**. Link in bio: **https://guide.delightdigital.online** (the rebranded funnel page).
