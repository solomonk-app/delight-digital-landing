## Core workbook — full structure (verified, 75pp)

- **Front matter:** Cover · Copyright/Responsible Use ("the fine print, in plain English") · Contents · Welcome ("You're not behind") · How to use ("read a little, do a little") · **Starting confidence check** (rate six skills, /30).
- **Part 1 — Your First Useful Week** (7 guided days + 7-day tracker): Day 1 explain something confusing · Day 2 improve a message · Day 3 plan a meal/task · Day 4 learn by conversation · Day 5 compare options · Day 6 voice or a document · Day 7 save your best prompt.
- **Part 2 — Build Better Prompts:** 11 ingredients (Role, Task, Audience, Format, Tone, Constraints, Context, Examples…) · 9 follow-ups · Three quiet habits · Prompt-building canvas · **The Prompt Doctor** (5 vague prompts fixed) · personalization "Voice Block."
- **Part 3 — Everyday AI Labs** (the heart, 9 areas): Home & Family · Communication · Work & Career · Learning · Money & Shopping · Travel & Planning · Healthcare Prep · Technology Help · Creativity & Personal.
- **Part 4 — Documents, Images & Voice:** read / see / hear, plus a Document Privacy checklist.
- **Part 5 — Getting Better Answers:** "when AI does this, say that" + **the Answer Rescue Kit (25 moves)**.
- **Part 6 — Trust, Privacy & Scams:** four things AI gets wrong · six scam red flags · **the PAUSE Check** · spot-the-red-flag practice.
- **Part 7 — Build Your Personal AI System:** three useful tasks · personal prompt library · weekly routine · "Keep Human" list · time-saved tracker · personal boundaries.
- **Part 8 — The 30-Day Confidence Plan:** 4 themed weeks (Ask & Improve · Everyday Life · Docs/Images/Voice · Verify & Build) + **re-score the six skills** (before/after).
- **Resource Library:** 50 everyday prompts · 25 follow-ups · "Which AI?" decision guide · verification & scam checklists · 2 cut-out cards · **completion certificate**.

**PAUSE Check (memorize-able framework):** **P**ause before acting · **A**sk where it came from · **U**se a second reliable source · **S**trip out sensitive info · **E**scalate to a qualified person.

## Website feature audit (verified in-browser)

**`workbook.delightdigital.online` — the fillable practice.** JWT-gated (access via purchase). Verified DOM: **134 checkboxes, 115 text inputs + 6 textareas** (write-in answers), **"Copy Prompt" buttons**, section **search**, **hamburger nav** (21+ section links), **PDF export** ("Print / Save whole workbook", "Print certificate only"), **136 activities**, **progress saved locally per-device** ("SAVED LOCALLY"), **responsive** (mobile viewport). Cream background. Day-by-day structure mirrors the PDF.

**`ai.delightdigital.online` — the reference guide.** Passcode-gated (passcode comes with the bundle); the free 16-pp guide is reachable without one. Sections: Start here (4 habits) · **Prompt recipe builder** (5 fields Role/Task/Audience/Format/Tone + chips + **copy** + "Open ChatGPT") · **3-question tool picker** · **12 tool cards** · **120-prompt library** (filter by 10 topics × who-it's-for; one-click **copy**; "Show 20 more") · "When to use which" table · 6 mistakes · **5 safety habits** · 8-Q FAQ · 10-term glossary · 6 free courses. Footer disclaimer: *"An educational guide · not affiliated with any tool listed · verify important facts with humans."*

**Difference, in plain words:** `ai.` = look things up & grab a prompt (reference). `workbook.` = do the 30-day practice & save it (fillable). Access is per-device/local, not a synced cloud account — say "saved on your device," not "syncs everywhere."

## The two-edition comparison (decision recorded)

`Not-Behind-Just-Beginning Bonus.pdf` (52pp, white/teal/gold) is an **earlier sibling edition**. It substantially duplicates the flagship (prompt recipe, 50+ prompts, use cases, safety, worksheets, a 7-day challenge). Its **only unique value**: 5 per-tool walkthroughs (ChatGPT/Claude/Gemini/Copilot/Perplexity), a ~26-term glossary, and 2 printable cheat sheets. **Decision: extract those into "Quick Reference & Tool Guide"; retire the rest.** Never market the two editions as two products.

## Volatile facts — do NOT quote in evergreen videos

Prices (~$20/mo), specific model names (GPT-4o, Claude Sonnet, Gemini Pro), Midjourney "$10+", "ChatGPT's image mode is free," and tool-routing "best at" claims all age fast (they appear in the "Which AI" sheet + tool pages). **Rule:** campaign videos teach evergreen habits and name tools only generically; if a video must cite a price/model, verify it that week or cut it.
