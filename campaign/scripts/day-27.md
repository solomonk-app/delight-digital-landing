## Final voiceover (91 words)
"What if starting with AI was just one tiny task a day, for seven days? That's the First Useful Week. Day one, ask it to explain something confusing. Day two, improve a message you're stuck on. Day three, plan a meal or a task. Day four, learn something by just chatting. Day five, compare two options. Day six, work with a photo or a document. Day seven, save your best prompt to keep. Seven small wins — five minutes each. No cramming, no catching up. By Sunday, using AI just feels normal."
