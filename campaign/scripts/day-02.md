## Final voiceover (96 words)
"If AI keeps giving you boring answers, it's probably not you — it's the question. Most of us type one line, like 'plan a workout,' and get something generic back. Good prompts have five little parts: who it should act as, the task, who the answer's for, the format, and the tone. Watch. 'Act as a gentle physiotherapist. A fifteen-minute morning workout for someone at a desk with a sore lower back. A numbered list, one rest day, warm and reassuring.' Same tool, completely different answer. Five small parts. That's the whole trick."
