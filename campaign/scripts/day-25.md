## Final voiceover (91 words)
"Got a text that feels a little off? Let's stay calm and check it together. Three red flags to look for. One: urgency — 'act now, your account closes today.' Two: secrecy — 'don't tell anyone, just handle this quietly.' Three: an odd way to pay — gift cards, wire transfers, crypto. If you see those, don't click and don't reply. You can even ask AI: 'here's a suspicious text — what are the red flags, and what should I do next?' Real messages can wait five minutes. Scams can't. When in doubt, slow down."
