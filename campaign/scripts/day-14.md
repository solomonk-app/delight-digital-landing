## Final voiceover (96 words)
"AI is safe to use — you just keep a few things to yourself, the same way you would with a stranger who's helping you. Five things to never paste in. One: passwords — a tool never needs them to answer a question. Two: one-time codes — those are keys to your accounts, meant for your eyes only. Three: full card or bank numbers — no everyday prompt requires them. Four: government ID numbers — describe your situation instead. Five: a login page — real sign-ins don't happen inside a chat. Keep those five out, and you're free to use the rest."
