# Day 09 — Decode a scary letter or form in plain English

- **Pillar:** 3 · Immediate-use demonstration
- **Format:** 5 · Everyday scenario + AI-generated B-roll
- **Bundle component featured:** Caregivers pack · Everyday AI Labs (read a document; Document Privacy checklist)
- **Objective:** Show AI turning an intimidating official letter into plain English and a clear next step — framed as preparation, never advice.
- **Target viewer:** Older adults and anyone who dreads jargon-filled letters and forms.
- **Primary goal:** Saves.
- **CTA (this video):** Educational — "Save it, then open a tool and paste it in. Report back." (from `06`)

## Final voiceover (94 words)
"A letter lands — insurance, a bill, something official — and it's a wall of jargon. Before you panic, let AI translate it. I open a tool and type: 'I'll paste a letter. Explain it in plain English, tell me what it's actually asking me to do, and list any questions I should ask. This is not legal or medical advice.' In seconds, the wall of words becomes plain sentences and a clear next step. It doesn't replace a professional — it just helps you walk in understanding what you're holding. Prepared, not panicked."
