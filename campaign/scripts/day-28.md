## Final voiceover (95 words)
"Here's an honest way to measure your AI progress — no hype, just you. Before you start, rate yourself on six small skills, each out of five. Things like: writing a clear prompt, spotting when AI might be wrong, keeping your private details private. Add them up — that's your starting score, out of thirty. Write it down and don't judge it. It's just a baseline. Then do the thirty-day plan, and at the end, score the same six again. Most of the change is quiet — you just notice you hesitate less. That number makes it visible."
