# Day 01 — "You're not behind, you're just beginning"

- **Pillar:** 2 · Relatable frustrations & fears
- **Format:** 4 · Text-led relatable story
- **Bundle component featured:** Permission Slip
- **Objective:** Open the campaign by relieving the core fear (being "behind"); earn saves + follows and set the calm brand tone.
- **Target viewer:** A complete beginner, ~35–60, who feels everyone else already understands AI.
- **Primary goal:** Saves / follows.

## Final voiceover (94 words)
"If you feel behind on AI — like everyone else got the memo and you missed it — I want to tell you something. You're not behind. You're just beginning. You don't have to learn everything. You don't have to use it every day. You don't have to catch up to anyone. You can start with one real question from your actual life — and that's enough. Open a tool, type one thing, read the answer. That's it. That's using AI. Beginning is not the same as being behind."
