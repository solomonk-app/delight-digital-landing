## Final voiceover (91 words)
"'Give me some birthday ideas.' That's what most of us type — and we get a boring, generic list back. Watch what happens when you add real details. 'Help me plan a small seventieth birthday for my mom. She loves gardening, jazz, and her grandkids, dislikes big crowds, budget of [X]. Suggest five low-stress ideas, each with a first step I could take this week.' Same tool. Now the answer actually fits your mom — and every idea comes with a first move. Real details in, useful answer out. That's the whole fix."
