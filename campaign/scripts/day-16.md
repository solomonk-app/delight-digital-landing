## Final voiceover (94 words)
"When you're caring for someone, the paperwork piles up — letters full of words you're scared to get wrong. AI can't replace the doctor, but it can help you walk in prepared. Try this: 'I'll paste a medical letter. Explain it in plain English, list anything I should ask the doctor, and flag words to look up. This is not legal or medical advice.' Now you understand the letter, you've got questions ready, and you know what to double-check. It doesn't decide anything. It just helps you show up ready to ask the right things."
