# Day 23 — Give it the backstory

- **Pillar:** 1 · Education
- **Format:** 2 · Before/after prompt (Prompt Doctor)
- **Bundle component featured:** Core workbook Part 2 — Build Better Prompts (Context ingredient / the three quiet habits)
- **Objective:** Teach the single highest-leverage habit after specificity — giving AI context — and prove it with a real before/after.
- **Target viewer:** Anyone getting stiff, generic answers who doesn't yet know why.
- **Primary goal:** Saves.
- **CTA (this video):** Educational — "Try this one prompt today — that's the whole assignment." (from `06`)

---

## Final voiceover (94 words)
"Here's the number-one fix for boring AI answers, and it takes one extra sentence. Give it the backstory. Watch the difference. Without context, I ask: 'Write a thank-you note.' I get something stiff and generic. Now with context: 'I'm a shy new employee. Write a short thank-you note to my manager who helped me through a hard first week. Warm, but not over-the-top.' Same tool, completely different answer — because now it knows who I am, who it's for, and what I want. Before you ask, spend one line on the backstory. It changes everything."
