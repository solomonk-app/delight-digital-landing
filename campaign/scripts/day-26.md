## Final voiceover (95 words)
"Dreading a hard conversation? You can rehearse it first — privately, with no one watching. Open a tool and tell it: 'Play the part of my [sister]. I need to talk about [sharing Mom's care]. Respond the way she might, and let's go about five turns.' Then just talk. It pushes back, you practice staying calm, you find better words. When it stalls, ask: 'what might I be missing?' You're not scripting the other person — you're warming up your own side. By the real conversation, it won't be the first time you've said it out loud."
