# Day 10 — "Which AI do I even use?"

- **Pillar:** 2 · Relatable frustrations & fears
- **Format:** 4 · Text-led relatable story
- **Bundle component featured:** "Which AI for Which Task" cheat sheet
- **Objective:** Dissolve tool-overwhelm; give permission to ignore the flood of apps and commit to a single tool to actually learn.
- **Target viewer:** A beginner who feels paralyzed by how many AI tools exist and can't decide where to start.
- **Primary goal:** Comments.
- **CTA (this video):** Educational — "Tell me: what stops you from trying AI? No wrong answers." (from `06`)

---

## Final voiceover (90 words)
"There are dozens of AI tools now, and every week someone launches another one. If that makes you want to close the tab — I get it. But here's the truth: you don't need all of them. You need one. Pick a single well-known tool, the one your friend keeps mentioning, and use only that for a month. Ask it one real question from your day. That's how you actually learn — not by collecting apps, but by using one until it feels normal. One tool. One question. That's the whole start."
