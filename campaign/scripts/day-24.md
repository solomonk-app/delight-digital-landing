## Final voiceover (90 words)
"Want your first real AI win? Give me five minutes. Open the workbook to Day One — the whole task is: ask AI to explain something confusing. Pick anything from your actual life — a letter, a term, a setting you don't understand. Type it in the box, and add: explain this in plain English, like I'm new to it. Read the answer. If a word's still fuzzy, ask again. Then tick Day One as done — it saves right on your device. That's it. Five minutes, one real question, and you've started."
