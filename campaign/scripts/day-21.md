## Final voiceover (94 words)
"Before you act on anything important an AI tells you, run one quick check. It spells PAUSE. P — pause before acting; don't rush a big decision on a first answer. A — ask where it came from; does the AI actually know, or is it guessing? U — use a second, reliable source to confirm. S — strip out sensitive info before you paste anything in. And E — escalate to a qualified person for anything medical, legal, or financial. Five letters. Ten seconds. It keeps AI as your helper, and keeps you as the one in charge."
