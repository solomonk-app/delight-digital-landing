## Final voiceover (92 words)
"Here's something nobody warns beginners about. AI can sound completely sure of itself — and still be wrong. It might invent a name, a date, a statistic, even a source that doesn't exist. That's called a hallucination. It's not lying; it's guessing, in a very confident voice. The fix is simple, and it puts you back in charge. Whenever a fact actually matters — a name, a number, a source — check it somewhere real before you use it. AI is a brilliant first draft, not the final word. You stay the one who decides."
