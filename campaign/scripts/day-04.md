## Final voiceover (89 words)
"It's five o'clock, the fridge is a bit random, and nobody planned dinner. This is my favorite thing to hand to AI. I open a tool like ChatGPT or Claude and type: 'I have [chicken, broccoli, rice, eggs] and about [30 minutes] to feed [a family of four]. Suggest three simple dinners, and mark the easiest one if I'm worn out.' A few seconds later I've got three real options — and it flags the low-effort one for the nights I'm done. No new groceries. No overthinking. Just dinner, sorted."
