## Final voiceover (95 words)
"When AI gives you an answer that's not quite right, most people just close the tab. But no answer is a dead end — it's a first draft you can steer. Watch. This reply is too long and generic. So I type: 'Make it shorter, in plain language.' Better. Then: 'Ask me three questions first, so it actually fits me.' Now it's mine. And one more: 'Tell me what I should double-check before I trust this.' There are twenty-five little moves like these. You never have to accept the first answer — you get to redirect it."
