## Final voiceover (96 words)
"People think using AI well is complicated. It's really just four small habits. One: talk to it like a person — plain, normal words, no special commands. Two: give it a little backstory — who you are, who the answer's for, what you actually want. Three: if the first answer misses, ask it to redo — shorter, warmer, whatever you need. Four: verify anything that matters — names, numbers, dates — with a real source. That's it. Talk like a person, give backstory, ask for a redo, verify. Four habits, and AI starts working for you."
