# Day 19 — "AI will take my job?" — a calmer, honest take

- **Pillar:** 2 · Relatable frustrations & fears
- **Format:** 4 · Text-led relatable story
- **Bundle component featured:** FAQ (the honest answers section)
- **Objective:** Meet the biggest quiet fear head-on with a balanced, honest answer that neither dismisses nor doom-mongers — and redirect it toward a learnable skill.
- **Target viewer:** An anxious beginner who half-avoids AI because it feels like a threat.
- **Primary goal:** Comments.
- **CTA (this video):** Educational — "Tell me: what stops you from trying AI? No wrong answers." (from `06`)

---

## Final voiceover (95 words)
"Let's be honest about the question everyone's quietly asking: will AI take my job? Here's a calm, truthful answer. Some tasks will change — that part is real, and pretending otherwise doesn't help anyone. But 'a task' and 'a job' aren't the same thing. What's becoming valuable isn't knowing everything; it's being the person who can ask AI good questions and check its work. That's a skill, and it's learnable — starting today, with one small task. You don't have to out-compute a machine. You just have to stay curious, and stay the human in the loop."
