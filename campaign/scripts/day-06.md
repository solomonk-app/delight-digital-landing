## Final voiceover (93 words)
"Here's what I wish someone told me sooner: the first answer is never the final answer. When AI gives you something that's close but not right, you don't start over — you just ask it to redo. Watch. 'Make it shorter.' 'Make it friendlier.' 'Give me three options instead of one.' Or my favorite: 'Ask me three questions first, then write it.' Suddenly it's tailored to you. Same chat, no retyping — you're just steering. The magic isn't the first reply. It's the follow-up. Treat it like a conversation, not a vending machine."
