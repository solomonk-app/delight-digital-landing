## Final voiceover (96 words)
"You know that page of messy notes after a meeting or a phone call? The scribbles you can barely read later? AI is genuinely good at this. Watch. I paste the whole messy block in, then I ask for three things: 'Summarize this in clear bullet points. List any decisions we made. And pull out the action items, with who's doing each one.' In seconds, the chaos becomes something I can actually use — and send to other people. Same messy notes, one tidy summary. Keep names and private details out, and let AI do the tidying."
