## Final voiceover (93 words)
"Every parent knows the supermarket meltdown. Aisle five, the trolley's half full, and suddenly your toddler is on the floor. You can't think fast in that moment — so rehearse it before it happens. Ask a tool like this: 'My two-year-old has tantrums in shops. Role-play a calm three-step script I can use in the moment, in plain words I'll actually remember.' It gives you the exact lines — get low, name the feeling, offer a small choice. You practice it once at home, and next time, you're not improvising. You've already got the words."
