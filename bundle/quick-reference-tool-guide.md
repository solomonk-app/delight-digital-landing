# Quick Reference & Tool Guide — build spec

**Purpose:** replace the 52-page `Not-Behind-Just-Beginning Bonus.pdf` in the customer bundle with a *slim* bonus made only of its genuinely unique, non-duplicated assets. This preserves the one thing a nervous beginner most wants — "show me exactly how to get *into* each tool" — without shipping two overlapping editions of the flagship.

> This is a **content spec / organizing doc**, not the final PDF. The source material lives in `../Not-Behind-Just-Beginning Bonus.pdf`. Final visual re-render (into the cream/espresso/terracotta brand system, to match the rest of the bundle) is a design-tool step for you or a designer. The 52-page file's current look is white/teal/gold and should be rebranded when re-rendered.
